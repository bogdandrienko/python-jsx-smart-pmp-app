# Unsubscribe from a stream

You can always unsubscribe from any stream in Zulip.

{start_tabs}

{!stream-actions.md!}

1. Click **Unsubscribe**.

{end_tabs}

## From the streams page (alternate method)

{start_tabs}

{relative|stream|subscribed}

1. Click on the green checkmark the left of the stream name.

{end_tabs}
